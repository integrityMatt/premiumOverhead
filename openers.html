Types of Garage Door Openers in Puyallup

The main types of garage door openers you can choose from include:

Chain Drive Openers

Chain drive openers use a metal chain and gears to lift the garage door. If your garage door is heavy, it’s a solid choice due to the strength and durability of the chain.

Belt Drive Openers

Belt drive openers use a rubber belt instead of a chain to lift the garage door. Compared to chain drive openers, they’re much quieter and are, therefore, a good option for attached garages.

Common Features of Garage Door Openers

Not all garage door openers are created equal. However, many modern options include these features and benefits you might want to look for.

  • Rolling code technology
  • Battery backup
  • Quiet operation
  • Keypad control
  • Smartphone connectivity and built-in WiFi
  • Motion-sensing security lights
  • Home automation system connectivity
  • Miniature keychain remotes

If you have an older garage door opener, consider upgrading to a new one to take advantage of these conveniences and improve the security of your garage door.
