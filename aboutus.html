About Us

Premium Overhead Garage Door is a company that specializes in garage door installation and repair services,family-owned, located in Puyallup, Washington. Since 2011, we have established ourselves as a provider of high-quality skills and exceptional customer service. Our team of experts is available 24/7 for emergency repairs and can handle any issues with your garage door.

We take pride in partnering with leading brands such as doorLink, Hormann, and Wayne Dalton, to provide our customers with access to the best products and technology in the industry. Additionally, we offer a Limited Lifetime Warranty on all of our installations, ensuring the durability of your garage door and providing you with peace of mind.

At Premium Overhead Garage Door, we strive to offer a seamless experience from start to finish, beginning with the initial consultation and culminating in the final installation or repair. Our commitment to providing the highest level of customer satisfaction sets us apart, and we make it a priority to ensure that each of our clients is completely satisfied with our services.

To schedule a consultation and discover the difference of working with the best in the business, contact us today.
